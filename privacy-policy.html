Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Radio Rainbow, accessible from our live streaming platform, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our listeners. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Radio Rainbow and how we use it.

1. Log Files & Analytics Telemetry

Radio Rainbow follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this as part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Radio Rainbow uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

3. Third-Party Players & Media Networks

Our platform routes its live audio feeds through third-party widget platforms (such as Zeno.FM). These third-party media networks or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons within their respective players and links that appear on Radio Rainbow. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their streaming delivery and/or to personalize the audio experience or contextual content that you hear or see.

Please note that Radio Rainbow has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party audio service providers.

4. Contact Form Information

When you contact us directly via our digital forms or email routes, we receive the fields you choose to supply (such as your name, email routing address, message contents, and attachments). This data is strictly used to process your inquiry, assist with show submissions, and improve station relations. We never sell, rent, or distribute this transmission information to external brokers.

5. Consent

By utilizing our online streaming infrastructure, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its operational terms.
